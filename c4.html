CROCK POT PEANUT CLUSTER CHOCLATE CANDY

Dessert pictures

Ingredients

  • 1 - 16 oz. container dry roasted, salted peanuts
  • 1 - 16 oz. container dry roasted, unsalted peanuts
  • 1 - 12 oz. bag semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 4 oz. German chocolate (or any other chocolate you have hanging around)
  • 32 oz. white almond bark

Directions

  1. Pour peanuts into the bottom of a crock pot. Put chocolate chips, German chocolate and almond bark on top.
  2. Cook on low, about 1 1/2 to 2 hours, or until chocolate is melted. Stir well.
  3. Drop by the tablespoon onto waxed-paper-lined cookie sheets.
  4. Let cool at room temperature or in the fridge until chocolate has hardened. Enjoy!
