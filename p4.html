APPLE COOKIE

Back home

Ingredients

  • 1cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/2cup butter or margarine, softened
  • 1egg
  • 1 1/2cups Gold Medal all-purpose flour
  • 1teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2teaspoon salt
  • 1/2teaspoon baking soda
  • 1medium apple, chopped (1 cup)

Directions

  1. Heat oven to 375. Grease cookie sheet with shortening. Beat brown sugar, butter and egg in large bowl with electric mixer on medium speed until light and fluffy. Stir in flour, nutmeg, salt and baking soda. Stir in apple.
  2. Drop dough by rounded teaspoonfuls about 2 inches apart onto cookie sheet.
  3. Bake 8 to 10 minutes or until light brown. Remove from cookie sheet to wire rack; cool. Store loosely covered.
